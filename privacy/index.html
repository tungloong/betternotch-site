Privacy Policy

BetterNotch works entirely on your Mac. Your wallpaper and usage stay yours.

English

BetterNotch renders its desktop effect entirely on your Mac. It does not create an account, show advertising, include analytics or tracking SDKs, collect personal data, or transmit wallpaper images or usage information to the developer or any third party.

The app stores only effect preferences and per-display style selections on your device. BetterNotch does not read, copy, modify, back up, or persistently bookmark wallpaper files.

Turning the effect off removes the overlay and leaves the system wallpaper unchanged. Removing the app and its container removes its locally stored preferences. BetterNotch has no third-party data processors and does not sell or share personal data.

If a future version changes these practices, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosure will be updated before that version is released.

Privacy questions: longbuild@icloud.com

简体中文

BetterNotch 完全在你的 Mac 本机渲染桌面效果。它不创建账户、不展示广告、不包含分析或追踪 SDK、不收集个人数据，也不会把壁纸图片或使用信息发送给开发者或任何第三方。

App 只在设备上保存功能所必需的信息：效果偏好和逐显示器样式选择。BetterNotch 不读取、复制、修改或备份壁纸文件，也不持久化壁纸授权书签。这些信息始终留在你的 Mac 上，不用于追踪。

关闭效果只会移除图层，系统壁纸保持不变。移除 App 及其容器会移除其本地偏好。BetterNotch 没有第三方数据处理方，也不会出售或共享个人数据。

如果未来版本改变上述做法，我们会在发布该版本前同步更新本政策和 App Store 隐私披露。

隐私问题：longbuild@icloud.com
