Privacy Policy

BetterNotch runs entirely on your Mac. It does not require an account or rely on any online service.

Data collection

BetterNotch does not collect, upload, or transmit personal data, usage data, or diagnostic data.

The app contains no advertising, analytics tools, or tracking SDKs. It does not create user profiles, sell data, or share data with third parties.

The public BetterNotch website stores only your selected language in your browser so the choice persists between pages and visits. This preference remains on your device and is not sent to the developer.

Information stored locally

To preserve your preferences, BetterNotch stores the following in the app’s local container:

  • Whether Desktop Effect is enabled
  • The selected style and intensity
  • Per-display settings
  • Whether the welcome guide has been completed

These settings remain only on your Mac and are not sent to the developer or any third party. They remain until replaced by newer settings or the app’s local container is deleted.

System permissions

BetterNotch uses public macOS APIs to display the effect at the top of the screen. If you choose to enable Launch at Login, macOS manages that setting.

The app does not request Screen Recording permission and does not read or record the windows or screen content of other apps.

Third-party services

BetterNotch has no servers, user accounts, third-party data processors, or external network services.

Policy updates

If a future version changes how data is handled, we will update this Privacy Policy and the App Store privacy disclosure before releasing that version.

Contact
