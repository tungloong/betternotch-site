专为带刘海的 MacBook 设计

让刘海隐入菜单栏。

BetterNotch 通过渐变与纯黑效果，尽可能弱化物理刘海的存在感，让屏幕顶部与菜单栏自然衔接。支持每块显示器独立设置，并以较低的资源占用持续运行。

01

自然衔接

弱化物理刘海的边界，让屏幕顶部看起来更加完整、协调。

02

逐屏设置

为内建显示器和每块外接显示器分别选择适合的效果。

03

轻量常驻

只处理屏幕顶部所需区域，适合长期在后台运行。

English

Designed for MacBook models with a notch

Let the notch fade into the menu bar.

BetterNotch uses gradient and solid-black effects to soften the presence of the physical notch as much as possible, helping the top of the display flow naturally into the menu bar. Each display can be configured independently, and the app is designed to run continuously with low resource use.

01

Natural blend

Softens the outline of the physical notch so the top of the display feels more complete and visually balanced.

02

Per-display control

Choose the right effect separately for the built-in display and each external display.

03

Lightweight by design

Works only where needed at the top of the screen, making it suitable for long-term background use.
